Learning English is One Click Away

Maverick, Ronn, Maron 2018

Our team, consisting of three developers namely Ronn, Maron, and Paul are sudents from Bulacan State University Laboratory High School. "Learning the fun way", we deviced an innovative learning website targeting children it is an interactive portal for simple english words. English is the universal language, so it is important for everyone including kids. Learning english will give them an advantage in school and especially in life. Philippines is a country that is proficient in the english language, by teaching the filipino children this language we can capitalize on this strength thus making a step forward into developing the country further.

    

    Our objective in making this website, V4K: Vocabulary for Kids is  First; to educate the young filipinos, equipping them with the capacity to adapt in diverse working places. Second, to create a user friendly medium that is free for everyone who has access to internet. Third, to help mothers, fathers, teachers and guardians in teaching the children about the basic words in the english vocabulary. Our vision is simple; filipino children being able to utter plain words that is used in daily conversations.

    To make it simple, we just want to develop a website that will cater the hunger for knowledge here in the Philippines

  1.  EDUCATION: to educate the young filipinos, equipping them with the capacity to adapt in diverse working places.
  2. USER FRIENDLY: to create a user friendly medium that is free for everyone who has access to internet.
  3. HELPFUL: to help mothers, fathers, teachers and guardians in teaching the children about the basic words in the english vocabulary.
